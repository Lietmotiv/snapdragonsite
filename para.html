About

Snapdragon Celebrations offers professional day-of-wedding and event planning. We will help make your big day more enjoyable by attending to all the last minute details, allowing you to focus on your guests and your once in a lifetime experience.

From flowers, to guest lists and table settings, Snapdragon can handle it all. Jill has years of experience, and will make your wedding or event in the Vail Valley an effortless success.

Be sure to call our office at 970-376-5320 or email us at snapdragoncelebrations@gmail.com to schedule a complimentary consultation with Jill.

Flowers

Flowers are an important part of celebrating and a great deal of time is taken to be sure the right color and types of flowers are selected. After the event many people throw their flowers away. Snapdragon has come up with a beautiful way to preserve your flowers to display with your photo memories. This is a unique keepsake for you, your parents, in-laws, wedding party, grandparents, family, and friends. It is also a great gift idea for a bride or groom!

We have all received meaningful flowers at one time in our lives and hung them to dry. Snapdragon has come up with a different way to create a unique memory with you flowers. We love all flowers and we started off sharing our work with our friends and family. Our production occurs in the beautiful Vail Valley where we can get out and appreciate all the natural flowers the earth offers. Now we would the opportunity to take your special flowers and share our work with you!

We have experimented with flower drying methods, flower pressing methods, paper making ingredients and more. We use only the highest quality products to produce archival quality paper with your flowers. We can use almost any flower to create your memory! Our vision is to share our love for creativity and nature with you!
