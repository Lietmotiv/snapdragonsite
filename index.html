About

Please call 970-376-5320 or email us at snapdragoncelebrations@gmail.com to schedule a complimentary consultation with Jill.

Owner Jill Chalfant loves weddings! She started in the wedding industry creating custom paper and photo mats from floral arrangements. This led to her realizing she had a real passion for the details of designing a memorable day. Jill thrives in the high energy environment of weddings and events, and that shows in the outcome of her planning. With 15 years of experience in the Vail area, she has the knowledge and relationships only a local can provide. She would be honored and excited to help you shape the perfect event.

Snapdragon Celebrations offers professional wedding and event planning services in the Vail/Beaver Creek area. From day-of-wedding coordination, to comprehensive planning, to designing and managing events, we will help make your experience more enjoyable by attending to all the details. We allow you to focus on your guests and your once-in-alifetime event.

Please contact us for more information about planning your intimate wedding or grand celebration. Snapdragon will work with you to find the right mix of elements to represent your vision to your friends and guests

Services

Full Planning Services

Excited to get married but don’t want to dig through lists of vendors and visit numerous locations? Weddings are now multi-day, multi-location events. Let us help you narrow down the choices based on your vision. We can create a custom wedding website, scout wedding and rehearsal locations, suggest photographers,florists, caterers, musicians and more. We can also help with designing invitations, programs, welcome bags, and decorations. If you need to know about local hair and makeup, accommodations, transportation, or just getting all the information to your guests, we are here for you. Paper

We make paper out of your flowers! Flowers are an important part of celebrating and a great deal of time is taken to be sure the right color and types of flowers are selected. After the event many people throw their flowers away. Snapdragon has come up with a beautiful way to preserve your flowers to display with your photo memories. This is a unique keepsake for you, your parents, in-laws, wedding party, grandparents, family, and friends. It is also a great gift idea for a bride or groom!

We have experimented with flower drying methods, flower pressing methods, paper making ingredients and more. We use only the highest quality products to produce archival quality paper with your flowers. We can use almost any flower to create your memory! Our vision is to share our love for creativity and nature with you!
